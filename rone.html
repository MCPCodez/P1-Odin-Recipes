Lasagna

by Mason Yates

Description

Are you ready to eat lasagna that tastes better than you coul ever imagine? Look no further this recipe is for you! Follow the directions below to enjoy this scrumpdidlyumptious meal!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Spices

Directions

  1. Boil water and add the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Add meat to a pan and cook it till done then strain and add spices during cooking.
  3. Add the pasta sauce and the tomato paste to the meat and stir as well as the garlic and parsley.
  4. Layer in a pan the meatsauce then mozzarella then noodle and rinse/repeat until the pan is full make sure you layer over and over.
  5. Enjoy the lasagna!
